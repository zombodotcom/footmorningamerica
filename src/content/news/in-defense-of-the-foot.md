Consider the foot. Down there. Doing its job. Asking for nothing but the occasional stretch and a clipped nail.

We celebrate the hands. We write songs about eyes. But the foot? The foot gets no parade. No holiday. No ticker-tape. Until now.

**Foot Morning America** stands for a simple idea: that the people who make foot content — the creators, the artists, the entrepreneurs of the arch — deserve a place to be found. Not hidden. Not buried in an algorithm. **Found.**

That's the whole mission. Discover them here. Support them there. Tip your feet to the people who put themselves out there.

It's a strange and beautiful world. Footy is honored to host it.

— Footy 🦶
